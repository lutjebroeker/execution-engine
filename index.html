How it works

Three rituals. One engine.

The Execution Engine runs on a simple daily loop — no dashboards to curate, no streaks to protect. Just three moments that keep you honest.

01
Morning Check-in

Every morning, your AI partner asks one question: what are your three priorities today, and how do they connect to your 12-week goal? No fluff. Three decisions. Full stop.

Daily · 5 min
02
Evening Reflection

At day's end, the engine asks what you completed — and what you didn't. If something slipped, it asks why. Not to judge. To pattern-match. Over time, you stop lying to yourself.

Daily · 5 min
03
Weekly Score

Every Sunday, your execution rate for the week is calculated. Not as a metric to optimize — but as a mirror. 87% means something. So does 34%. The number doesn't lie.
