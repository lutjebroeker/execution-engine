## Quarter Theme

Build the foundation — ship one product, establish daily review habit.

## 12-Week Goals

1. Launch first digital product
2. Establish daily morning review habit (Telegram check-in every day)
3. Clear outstanding project backlog

## Results

1. Product launched in week 10 — 3 pre-sales before public release
2. Daily review streak: 68 of 84 days (81% execution rate)
3. Backlog cleared by week 8 — ahead of schedule

## Lessons

The Telegram check-in is what made the habit stick. Having a system that asks you the question is more powerful than trying to remember to do it yourself. The days I missed were all travel days — build a lightweight travel version.

## Next Quarter

Scale the product to 10 buyers. Build travel-mode morning routine. Start evening review habit.
